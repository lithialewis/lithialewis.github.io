A very short guide on how to write songs

Kinda like music theory 101 but shorter. Sep 28th, 2024

Okay so like first you need an idea or a concept in your mind (e.g what it's about blah blah). Idk how to explain so here's an in detail guide

Step 1: find your inspiration

Every song starts out with a feeling. Maybe it's the goofy melody from a retro game or randomly playing notes on instruments. Wharever it is, start with a feeling, it helps the song shape it's form :3c

Step 2: pick the instruments

Yeah so you just kinda pick the instruments based on the feeling of your song.

Step 3: melody and chords

There isn't strict rules to melody or which scale you should play (unless you're writing a classical styled piece)
Just a few important things: major is happy is minor is sad (not always)

Step 4: experimenting

Fool around !! Mix and mash the chords, melody, instruments to create the perfect song that you dreamt of. This step is mostly to refine your song =3
